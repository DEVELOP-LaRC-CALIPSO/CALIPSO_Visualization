Generating releases for VOCAL with cx_freeze:
---------------------------------------------
  * cd into vocal/calipso
  * python setup.py build_exe
  * copy all numpy libs into folder of generated executable
    - libifcoremd.dll
    - libifportmd.dll
    - libiomp5md.dll
    - libiompstubs5md.dll
    - libmmd.dll
  * cd to location of executable, modify tcl/init & tk/tk
    - change 'package require -exact Tcl 8.5.15' to 'package require -exact Tcl 8.5.18'
